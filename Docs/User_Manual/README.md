Objectives: The Golden Eagle Flight Plan is an application that helps university students track their four-year academic and professional development journey in one centralized platform. Currently, students must juggle multiple disconnected sources for campus resources and advising: emails, social media, various websites, and one-off meetings with advisors. This application solves that problem by bringing everything together: a gamified scoreboard system that tracks student progress across Academic Preparation, Career Development, and Community & Leadership; a personalized resources and events feed that surfaces content relevant to each student's major, year, and interests; and a faculty-facing dashboard that allows advisors to post targeted content and monitor individual student progress. The goal is to graduate well-rounded, career-ready, and community-engaged students by making growth visible and actionable for both students and faculty.
